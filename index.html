Aristole Onassis

1906-1975

One of the richest people in the 20th century

Aristole socrates onassis (Greek:Aristolells Onasis, 20 Jan 1906 - 15 March 1975), commonly called Ari or Aristo onassis, was a Greek-Argetine shipping magnate, who amasses the world' largest privately owned shipping fleet and was one of the world's richest and most famous men.
